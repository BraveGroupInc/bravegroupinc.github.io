Hi, my name is Ben, and I play the alto saxophone.

I have always loved playing the alto saxophone. As a child, I vividly remember listening to old recordings of Greg Abate and being amazed. Growing older, this childhood passion grew within me as I began to learn the alto sax. I have dedicated my life to making the alto sax the expressor of my creative juices. From concerts to auditions to studios, my alto sax and I have been to a myriad of heights together, and, as a result, my alto sax is forever engrained within me. I'd like to thank you for visiting my website. Explore everything I have to offer. Enjoy!
